## 4. Automated Viral Clipping Workflow (Core Feature)
- **/process/[project_id]**: AI Analysis view (Transcription & Hook detection progress)
- **/curation/[project_id]**: 
    - **Clip Gallery**: Grid of AI-suggested viral segments with "Viral Scores"
    - **Review Interface**: Side-by-side transcript and video preview
- **/editor/[clip_id]**:
    - **Subtitle Engine**: Edit Thai captions, change fonts/styles
    - **Re-framing**: 9:16 crop adjustment
    - **Templates**: Viral style presets (Thai Subtitle layouts)
