# Strategic Roadmap: Thai Creator AI Platform (2024-2025)
**Project Vision:** To become the #1 AI partner for Thai creators by mastering the nuances of Thai language and cultural virality.

## Phase 1: Foundation & Thai Linguistic Dominance (Q4 2024)
- **Objective:** Establish the most accurate Thai Speech-to-Text (STT) engine.
- **Key Actions:**
    - Fine-tune Whisper-v3 models with 10,000+ hours of Thai colloquial speech, slang, and "Thanglish."
    - Develop "Natural Thai Segmentation" (Smart line breaks for Thai script without spaces).
    - Build MVP: Long-video upload -> Auto-transcribe -> Manual trim.

## Phase 2: Viral Intelligence & Automation (Q1 2025)
- **Objective:** Automate the "Hook" detection based on Thai social media trends.
- **Key Actions:**
    - Train "Thai Viral Scoring" algorithm using engagement data from Top 1000 Thai TikTok/Reels accounts.
    - Implement Auto-Face Tracking and "Thai-Style" dynamic captions (localized fonts like Kanit, Prompt).
    - Beta launch for 500 selected Thai influencers.

## Phase 3: Monetization & Ecosystem Integration (Q2 2025)
- **Objective:** Bridge the gap between content and commerce.
- **Key Actions:**
    - Integrate Thai Affiliate links (Shopee/Lazada) automatically based on video context.
    - Launch "Team Collaboration" features for Thai production houses.
    - Multi-platform auto-posting (TikTok, FB Reels, YT Shorts).

## Phase 4: Market Dominance & Expansion (Q3 2025 - Beyond)
- **Objective:** Scaling and AI-driven content generation.
- **Key Actions:**
    - AI Voice Cloning (Thai) for automated voiceovers.
    - Expansion into neighboring SE Asian markets (Lao, Khmer) using similar linguistic logic.
